This app allows you to keep the track of your scores from all academic years by providing you with a graphical view of your progress, subject-wise and term wise.

What is Result Tracker?

Result Tracker is an app that collects the data you enter regarding your terms, marks, subjects and provide you with a graphical representations of each subject's score for better understanding of the data. It analyses your scores in each subject and provides you with information like, what are your best performance and in which subjects your performance is below average. It keeps all your data in a systematically organized way, where only you can access the information by providing correct Username or Email and Password.

How the Result Tracker app can be useful for students?

Result Tracker app provides you with an over-view of your performance throughout a term, irrespective of the user being a school student or a college student. It provides you with the freedom to choose your term name, subject name and exam structure as well. So any student can enter the required details and enjoy the wonderful functionalities of the application.
